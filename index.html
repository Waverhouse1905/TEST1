Welcome to the Bavarian Neoliberal Club!

What is the Bavarian Neoliberal Club?

The Bavarian Neoliberal Club is a privately financed organization representing Neoliberal values.
Our Headquarters are based in Bavaria, Germany, but we have since branched out into many different countries.
As of 2020, we have over 20.000 members world wide.

Our Mission Statement

We seek to further our understanding of Neoliberalism through heated debate and close study of recent emperical research
Anyone is welcome to join our organization or simply participate in our debates.
This is because, we recognize that free speech is not just another value such as benevolence or loyalty.
Free speech is the central mechanism holding together our civilization by making it possible to refine our ideas in civil debate.
